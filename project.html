About Us

It all started with a young man named Max, who inherited his grandfather's old burger joint. Max wasn't a fancy chef, but he possessed an almost mystical ability to blend the perfect patty—a secret blend of locally sourced beef, a hint of garlic, and a touch of his grandmother's special spice. The burgers were cooked on a vintage grill, the flames dancing a rhythmic ballet as Max flipped each patty with practiced ease.
